My Very Own Tomagatchi!


How to Play

It's fun and easier than ever before to play with your very own Tomagatchi! But we can't forget they are more than just pets, they have feelings too!

So, it's important to know how to take care of your Tomagatchi! They have 3 key attributes

  • Hunger: Don't let them starve, but don't overfeed them either! Tomagatchi's love food too much!
  • Fatigue: How tired they are! They need sleep to be healthy, but too much sleep is bad for them too!
  • Energy: How playful and bored your Tomagatchi is! If it's too low they can get sick, but if it's too high they can misbehave too!
